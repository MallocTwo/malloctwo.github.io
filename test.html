ATA Settlement Creation

  1. Login into ATA webiste
  2. Click on Food tab
  3. Click on Camp Ops
  4. Instead of downloading PDF download CSV
  5. Click Choose File on this website
  6. Click on the CSV you downloaded from the ATA website
  7. Click on Upload
  8. Open the zip folder that was downloaded
  9. Done
